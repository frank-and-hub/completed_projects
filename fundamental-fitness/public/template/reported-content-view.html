Sea Animals

Fold a square sheet of paper diagonally into a triangle, shape the body by folding the corners up, and angle the sides to form the tail. Add decorations, and your origami fish is ready!

Create a Flower.

Fold a square paper in half diagonally, then fold the edges to the center. Flip it and repeat. Lift the corners to form petals, and add a stem to complete your origami flower!
